# Exhibitions to Add to Sanity Studio

To access Sanity Studio and add these exhibitions:
1. Run `npm run dev` in the terminal
2. Navigate to `http://localhost:3000/studio` in your browser
3. Click on "Exhibition" in the left sidebar
4. Click "+ Create" button to add each exhibition below

**Country:** China

## 2. International Art Conference of BIAB

**Title:** The 1st International Art Conference of BIAB

**Slug:** `biab-conference-2025`

**Theme/Subtitle:** Mutual Learning Among Civilizations and Artistic Innovation

**Start Date:** 2025-12-29

**End Date:** 2025-12-30

**Venue:** Beijing Exhibition Hall

**City:** Beijing

**Country:** China

**Exhibition Type:** Forum/Conference

**Description:**
On the occasion of the 10th Beijing International Art Biennale (BIAB), the 1st international Art Conference of BIAB with the theme "Mutual Learning Among Civilizations and Artistic Innovation", was convened. This extends from the visual presentation of exhibitions to deeper intellectual and cultural exchange, gathering global artistic forces to explore new pathways for art development.

Themed "Mutual Learning Among Civilizations and Artistic Innovation," it featured three sessions for discussion. The second session, "Fluid Contemporaneity and the Construction of Cultural Subjectivity," explores the cross-cultural characteristics of contemporary art under globalization, analyzes the interaction between global vision and localized practice, and examines how countries sustain indigenous cultural traditions through artistic innovation.

**Role:** Participating Artist & Roundtable Speaker

**Session:** Fluid Contemporaneity and the Construction of Cultural Subjectivity

Ledia Çeta was invited as one of 8 roundtable discussion speakers, alongside scholars from the United States, Germany, France, Korea, and China.

## 3. Everything Has a Spirit

**Title:** Everything Has a Spirit

**Slug:** `everything-has-a-spirit-2025`

**Theme/Subtitle:** International Environmental Art Exhibition

**Start Date:** 2025-12-09

**End Date:** 2025-12-10

**Venue:** Nansha

**City:** Guangzhou

**Country:** China

**Exhibition Type:** Group Exhibition

**Description:**
Organised by China Economic Information Agency and supported academically by the Sculpture Department of Tsinghua University School of Fine Arts, the "Everything has a Spirit" environmental protection-themed art pop-up event, held in Nansha, Guangzhou.

The exhibition was positioned on "art intervention ecology and aesthetic activation action", integrating ecological concepts into public spaces so that people can deeply experience the excellent chemical effect generated by art supporting environmental protection.

The artwork entitled "The Sea Before the Storm", has been exhibited along with international masterpieces from Picasso, Dali, and other renowned artists.

**Participating Artwork:** The Sea Before the Storm (Watercolours on Silk, 50×50 cm, 2025)

**Artist Statement (from Xinhua News Agency interview):**
"My hometown is nestled by the Adriatic Sea, where crystal-clear waters kiss the reefs and fishing boats sway gently on the shimmering waves. The ocean is the very inspiration behind my creations. When I witnessed plastic waste defiling the blue waves and oil slicks corroding the corals, I realized deeply that the ocean is not an inexhaustible treasure, but a living being that demands our protection. Every one of my marine paintings captures its grandeur while voicing its fragility."

## 6. Melody - Contemporary Art Exhibition

**Title:** Melody

**Slug:** `melody-2025`

**Theme/Subtitle:** Contemporary Art Exhibition

**Start Date:** 2025-06-28

**End Date:** 2025-08-17

**City:** Shenzhen

**Country:** China

**Exhibition Type:** Group Exhibition

**Description:**
LEDIA CETA is a remarkable female artist from Albania, with over 30 years of experiences from her journeys. As the wife of a diplomat, Ledia brings her passion for art and resilience to this vibrant and creative land of China.

During the four years living in China, she has been going on vacation back to Albania and has been inspired by the Sea and coastline of Albania and has created a number of works on this subject. These works are not only distilled from the images of the sea, it is also a reflection of her inner feelings. Through the wave of the color and crisscross of lines she shows the wave of the ocean and her passion for the life. Each work is a revelation of her inner world, each painting is a reflection of her soul.

Her work has a unique artistic charm and profound meaning, conveying in the words of art a profound meditation on nature and life. Her work allows viewers to feel a spiritual resonance beyond the physical world. It is as if you are in a dream of transcendent reality. In her work, one can find inner peace and serenity, and savor the beauty of indisputableness.

Ledia's artistic masterpiece have stunningly appeared in exhibitions such as "Transformation of Dragons and Snakes 2025." Accompanied and praised by the audience, her work not only reflects her unique understanding of the ocean, but her deep love of the nature and life.

**Exhibited Artwork:** 16 paintings including "At the Seaside" (Acrylic on Canvas, 60 x 50 cm, 2025)

**Start Date:** 2025-03-01

**End Date:** 2025-03-30

**Venue:** Hebei Normal University Museum, Zhengding Museum, Guan Shan Art Museum

**City:** Hebei

**Country:** China

**Exhibition Type:** Invitational

**Description:**
Organized by: Hebei Provincial Department of Culture and Tourism, Hebei Normal University
Curator: Professor Cui Qiang, dean of the School of Fine Arts and Design at Hebei Normal University, and Professor Wang Chunchen, former deputy director of the Art Gallery of the Central Academy of Fine Arts

The exhibition brought together 151 outstanding artists from 12 countries, with works in ink, oil painting, prints, sculpture, and installation art and video. The artists responded to the theme of "The Changing of the Dragon and the Snake" in a multifaceted way, jointly interpreting the symbiotic relationship between traditional and contemporary. The exhibition aims to use art as a way to explore the relationship between traditional and contemporary, East and West, and the transformation and growth of traditional art genes in contemporary contexts.

"It was a pleasure to be invited to participate at this exhibition as an artist. Each of my works has the theme of the sea, representing the sea in my country. In this exhibition, I was very pleasantly surprised to see this high level of performance of Chinese contemporary art. All the works are very good."

## Instructions for Adding to Sanity:

1. Open Sanity Studio at http://localhost:3000/studio
2. Navigate to "Exhibition" from the left sidebar
3. Click "+ Create" to add a new exhibition
4. Copy and paste the information from each exhibition above
5. For the Description field, paste the text and format it as needed
6. Add poster images if you have them
7. Link participating artworks by creating the artwork entries first, then referencing them
8. Save each exhibition

**Note:** Make sure to create artwork entries for referenced pieces like "Under the Sea", "The Sea Before the Storm", "Fire", etc., before linking them to exhibitions.
